# Bulk discount pricing
> Rules for bulk pricing discount.

## Scenario: no discount for orders under 10 items
* Total for "9" items at unit price "10" should be "90"

## Scenario: 10% discount applies at 10 items
* Total for "11" items at unit price "10" should be "99"

## Scenario: 20% discount applies at 100 items
* Total for "100" items at unit price "10" should be "800"
